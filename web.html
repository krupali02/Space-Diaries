HELLO THERE

Hey there! My name is Krupali Patel and I am a first-year computer science student currently attending York University.

Here's a little bit more about me. I currently live in Vaughan, Ontario with my family and I am truly passinate about coding, art, and space. So I thought, why not combine those three passions and make something out of it. As a result, I created this mini-blog post.

Through my knowledges in a few coding languages (specifically JavaScript, HTML and CSS), I combined it with my visual style to create the basic design and functions. For the actual content component, I wanted to find a way to combine my artictic style with space. As a result, for each planet and star mentioned in this site, I put my own visual twist. You will be able to find an actual photo of the planet or star and then beside it, should be my on take, in the form of a drawing or painting.
